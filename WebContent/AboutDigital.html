About us



In India there are more than 6,35,000 villages, which are represented by 2,50,000 Panchayats and more than 3,00,000 Panchayat functionaries, which is an enromous number to represent over 70 percent of population of India that lives in rural parts of the country. And There is practically no such governance system in these constituencies for sharing information related to schemes which are provided by government to reach out to its maximum citizens.In order to address these issues, Digital Gram Panchayat Website is an initiative for the rural sector providing comprehensive software solution attempting automation of Gram Panchayat functions.The features of Digital Gram Panchayat Website are to give complete information about the schemes which are provided by the government and pay taxes.
